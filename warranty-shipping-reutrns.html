Shipping, Returns & Warranty

Shipping Policy

We aim to provide fast and reliable shipping to all our customers. Below are the details:

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Shipping Methods: We offer standard and express shipping options.
  • Estimated Delivery Times: Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days locally.
  • Shipping Costs: Free shipping for orders above E500. A flat rate applies for smaller orders.
  • Order Tracking: A tracking number will be emailed once your order is shipped.

Returns Policy

We understand that sometimes a return may be necessary. Our return policy includes:

  • Return Eligibility: Items must be returned within 30 days, unused, and in their original packaging.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Personalized or custom-made items are non-returnable.
  • Return Process: Email us at Lucttechsupport@gmail.com with your order details to initiate a return.
  • Return Shipping Costs: Customers are responsible for return shipping fees unless the item is defective.
  • Refund Timeline: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the item is received.

Warranty Policy

Our products come with a warranty to ensure quality and satisfaction:

  • Warranty Coverage: Covers manufacturing defects for 1 year from the date of purchase.
  • Exclusions: Warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or unauthorized repairs.
  • How to Claim: Contact us at Lucttechsupport@gmail.com with proof of purchase and photos of the defect.
  • Repair or Replacement: We will repair or replace defective items free of charge if covered under warranty.

Damaged Goods

If your order arrives damaged, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged product. We will assist you with a replacement or refund.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping, returns, or warranties, please feel free to contact us at Lucttechsupport@gmail.com.
